About Upaman Financial Calculators

Our Mission

To provide accurate, easy-to-use financial calculators that help individuals make informed financial decisions across different countries and contexts.

What We Offer

Upaman Financial Calculators is a comprehensive suite of financial tools designed for both Indian and European markets:

🇮🇳 Indian Financial Tools

  • EMI Calculator: Calculate loan EMIs with detailed amortization schedules
  • GST Calculator: Comprehensive GST calculations with reverse GST features
  • Income Tax Calculator: Calculate Indian income tax with latest slabs
  • SIP Calculator: Mutual fund SIP return calculations
  • PPF Calculator: Public Provident Fund maturity and yearly projection estimates
  • Salary Calculator: In-hand salary calculations from CTC
  • Credit Card Trap Calculator: Minimum due vs fixed payment payoff comparison
  • IRCTC Calculator: Train fare calculations and booking insights

🇪🇺 European Financial Tools

  • UK Income Tax Calculator: British tax calculations with current rates
  • European VAT Calculator: Multi-country VAT calculations
  • European Salary Calculator: Net salary calculations across EU countries

🌍 Universal Tools

  • Age Calculator: Precise age calculations with detailed breakdowns

Why Choose Upaman?

  • Multi-Country Support: Specialized tools for different countries and tax systems
  • User-Friendly Design: Clean, intuitive interface that works on all devices
  • Completely Free: No hidden charges, no subscriptions, no registration required

We are committed to providing accurate, up-to-date financial calculations. However, please note that our tools are for informational purposes only. Always consult with qualified financial advisors for important financial decisions.

Technology
