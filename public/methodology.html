This page documents core formulas, assumptions, and limitations used in Upaman calculator tools. Results are informational estimates and not legal, tax, investment or accounting advice.

Scope

  • Indian calculators: EMI, SIP, PPF, GST, Income Tax, Salary, Credit Card Trap, IRCTC booking utility, Age.
  • US calculators: Mortgage, Refinance, Auto Loan, 401(k), Savings/CD, Credit Card Payoff.
  • EU/UK calculators: UK Income Tax, EU VAT, European Salary, Germany/France/Netherlands salary, UK Rail utility.
  • Guided workflows: Job Offer Decision and Home Loan Readiness (region-aware models).

Formula Highlights

Loan EMI: EMI = P*r*(1+r)^n / ((1+r)^n - 1), where r = annualRate/12.

SIP future value: monthly compounding with optional step-up contributions.

PPF projection: contribution-mode-based monthly accrual approximation with annual interest crediting.

GST add/remove: add uses amount * rate; reverse uses inclusive * 100 / (100 + rate).

Tax calculators: slab-wise marginal application with cess/rebate logic as configured in each tool.

Credit card trap: month-wise amortization using APR/12, minimum due guardrails and fixed-payment comparison.

Salary tools: approximate structures and deduction assumptions are explicitly listed in each calculator.

Guided Workflow Models

Job Offer Decision Workflow: compares current vs new annual compensation, estimates annual tax and local deductions with region presets, and adjusts monthly gain by location cost index. Output includes affordability-based allocation suggestions and decision flags.

Home Loan Readiness Workflow: computes safe housing budget using risk profile (FIOR cap + buffer), derives safe EMI after estimated ownership overhead, and compares against required EMI for target property and tenure.

Regions supported: India, United States, EU/UK (generic). Region changes affect currency format, default assumptions, and recommendation thresholds.

Data and Update Policy

  • Rules and rates are updated when official policy announcements are confirmed.
  • Market/news widgets rely on periodic static JSON updates and external data feeds where applicable.
  • UK rail utility may use live API data or controlled fallback responses when API access is unavailable.

Known Limitations

  • Regional workflow models are planning approximations and not substitutes for country-specific tax filing or lender underwriting.
  • Jurisdiction-specific exceptions, special exemptions, and edge-case tax situations may not be fully represented.
  • Real lender products, fund performance, and statutory guidance can differ from model assumptions.
  • Users should validate critical decisions using official portals and licensed professionals.
