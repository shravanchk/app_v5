Purpose

Upaman publishes free calculator tools and explanatory content for informational use. Our objective is accuracy, clarity, and transparent assumptions.

Standards

  • Formulas are implemented consistently and tested for representative scenarios.
  • Tax rate or policy references are updated after official announcements are confirmed.
  • Pages include limitations and disclaimers to avoid overclaiming precision.
  • We avoid publishing personalized financial, legal, or tax advice.

Update Cadence

  • Policy-sensitive pages are reviewed around major budget/tax updates.
  • Static market/news data files are refreshed periodically per project operations.
  • Page-level metadata and internal links are maintained to support discoverability.

Corrections

If a factual or calculation issue is identified, we prioritize a correction patch and redeploy. Material changes are reflected in page update timestamps where appropriate.

Independence

Content is educational. Monetization (for example ads or affiliate blocks) does not alter formula logic or force a specific financial recommendation.
